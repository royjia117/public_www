Geometry and Mechanics of Soft Matter

The mechanics of thin flexible structures, such as the membranes that envelop our cells, are intimately tied to their geometry. How can we exploit this coupling to design structures that take on a certain desirable conformation?

Active fluid dynamics

In biology it is rare for objects to be sitting still. The physics of active systems composed of many interacting agents far from equilibrium, such as flocks of birds or schools of fish, exhibits a cornucopia of rich and interesting dynamical phenomena.
