Leroy Jia

I am a Flatiron Research Fellow in Michael Shelley's Biophysical Modeling Group at the Flatiron Institute's Center for Computational Biology . Prior to my current appointment, I received my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics in 2018 from Brown University, where I was advised by Profs. Thomas Powers (Engineering & Physics) and Robert Pelcovits (Physics). My research focuses on the applied analysis and geometry of soft, fluid, and active matter.
